Whitepaper

🌐 Introduction

RugSafe is a Solana-based meme token with utility features built to inspire trust, transparency, and decentralization. With secure buy/sell tax handling, visible burn mechanisms, and complete community control, it stands as a new generation of safe crypto projects.

🔁 Token Utility

The RugSafe token is designed to reward holders while supporting community-driven growth. Future utility includes a leaderboard, burn logs, and gated perks based on holdings.

💸 Buy & Sell Tax

RugSafe can feature a minimal 0.5% buy/sell tax in future, ensuring price stability while funding development and emergency reserves.

🔥 Burn Mechanism

On launch, 300 million tokens will be burned, ensuring reduced circulating supply and long-term value appreciation. Burn logs will be transparent and public on the "Burned Tokens" page.

🎁 Community Engagement

Giveaways, airdrops, and leaderboard-based bonuses will strengthen the community. Early users and contributors will be rewarded as RugSafe grows.

🔐 Anti-Rug Features

Contract will be renounced, LP will be locked (or burned when feasible), and no single wallet will hold more than 10% of supply outside dev allocation. All dev actions will be transparent.

🗺️ The Future

RugSafe aims to expand into partnerships, utility NFTs, leaderboard bounties, and staking pools. Future DAO voting and governance is planned to hand over control to the community.
